Decision Integrity Systems

Klyntel builds control infrastructure that determines when domain-specific AI models are safe to trust in production environments.

What It Is

A runtime integrity layer that sits between your domain-specific AI models and production systems. It continuously evaluates model reliability, surfaces uncertainty, and gates actions when trust degrades.

How It Works

Decision integrity systems monitor the gap between what your model assumes and what your environment actually looks like. When distributions shift, correlations break, or uncertainty grows beyond acceptable thresholds, the system intervenes.

Core capabilities include:

  • Real-time domain drift detection using business-relevant metrics
  • Uncertainty quantification grounded in operational context
  • Automated decision gating based on configurable risk thresholds
  • Observability into model trust states and intervention events

Integration

Deploys as a lightweight service layer. Integrates with existing ML infrastructure through standard APIs. Compatible with any model framework or serving platform.

Use Cases

Built for organizations running domain-specific models in critical workflows:

  • Dynamic pricing and revenue optimization
  • Recommendation and personalization systems
  • Automated underwriting and risk assessment
  • Supply chain and demand forecasting
  • Fraud detection and anomaly monitoring
